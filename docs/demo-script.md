# ApplyMate AI — 2-Minute Demo Script

> Before demoing: open the Control Center and click **"↺ Reset demo data"** so the checklist and queue start fresh. Language can be switched to EN/TR/DE at any point via the header or sidebar pills.

## Walkthrough

**1. Landing page (`/`) — ~15s**
Scroll the hero and the "AI prepares. You approve. It tracks." section.
> *"ApplyMate is a job application operating system. One profile in — and the AI scans jobs, hides low-fit roles, and prepares complete application packages. The key: **nothing is ever sent without your approval.**"*

**2. Control Center (`/dashboard`) — ~20s**
Point at the trust metrics row and the review-first mode panel.
> *"This is the engine room. 1,240 jobs scanned, 45 low-fit hidden automatically — and this number here is the whole product: **0 sent without review.** There is no auto mode. AI prepares, you approve."*
Point at the **Getting started** checklist: *"The app walks a new user through the whole flow."*

**3. Profile + Master CV Preview (`/profile`) — ~20s**
Scroll to Master CV Preview, click **Generate**.
> *"You build one profile, once. ApplyMate turns it into a Master CV foundation — with a readiness score, gaps, and concrete improvements. Every application package is tailored from this."*

**4. Review Queue (`/review-queue`) — ~15s**
Show a card; use arrow keys.
> *"The AI only queues jobs above your 75% match threshold. Approve, skip, or decline — keyboard-fast."*

**5. Application Package (`/review?job=N`) — ~20s**
Click **"Review application →"** on a card.
> *"This is what makes ApplyMate different from a bot: a full package — tailored cover letter, CV adaptation notes, recruiter message, risk analysis, interview prep — assembled for your review, not sent behind your back."*

**6. Approve — ~5s**
Click **✓ Approve & apply**.
> *"I approve it — and only now does it move forward."*

**7. Tracker (`/tracker`) — ~15s**
Show the approved card in the Applied column ("New" chip).
> *"Approved packages land in the pipeline: applied, reply pending, follow-up due, interview, archived. Each card links back to the exact package I approved."*

**8. Inbox (`/inbox`) — ~15s**
Select the reply message; show the draft.
> *"Replies come back to one inbox. ApplyMate triages them and drafts responses — but look at the wording: **prepared for your review.** Same rule everywhere: it never sends an email without you."*

## Key phrases to repeat

- **"0 sent without review."**
- **"AI prepares. You approve. It tracks."**
- "Application **packages**, not blind submissions."
- "Quality-first, not volume-first."
- *"Let AI handle the applications. You focus on improving your profile, skills, and interviews."*

## Honesty notes (if asked)

- Frontend demo: no real submissions, no real AI calls yet; content is realistic mock data.
- Decisions persist locally (localStorage) — reset anytime from the Control Center.
